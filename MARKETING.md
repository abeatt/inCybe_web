# 📢 inCybe Marketing Strategy

## "Making simple tech"

## 🎯 Objective

To position inCybe as the premier local-first, privacy-preserving AI operating layer, moving users from passive data storage to active knowledge synthesis.

## 💎 Brand Pillars

1. **Sovereignty:** Your data never leaves your hardware.
2. **Legacy:** Building a system that evolves over decades, not subscription cycles.
3. **Simplicity:** Removing the friction between thought and digital organisation.

## 🚀 Phase-Based Campaign

### Phase 1: "The Foundation" (Current)

**Focus:** Obsidian Integration & Local RAG.

* **Content:** "Why Local LLMs are the future of Note Taking."
* **Channels:** Obsidian Forums, r/ObsidianMD, r/LocalLLaMA.
* **Hook:** Showcase the "Vault Reader" resolving complex wikilinks locally.

### Phase 2: "The Collaborator"

**Focus:** AI-Assisted Note Management.

* **Content:** "Stop organising, start thinking."
* **Visuals:** Demonstrations of the "Safe Write System" performing bulk metadata updates with user approval.
* **Hook:** The peace of mind provided by atomic writes and version history.

### Phase 3: "The AI Operating Layer"

**Focus:** Intelligent AI Operating Layer.

* **Content:** "Folders as suggestion, not law."
* **Hook:** Finding files by *meaning* across the entire OS using the Semantic Capsule Protocol (SCP).

## 📮 Communication Channels

### 📣 Marketing & Socials

* **X / Instagram / TikTok:** High-level vision, 4Data clips, and "Simple Tech" tips.
* **LinkedIn:** Business updates and Giblets Creations toolchain deep dives.
* **Reddit:** Targeted posts in r/LocalLLaMA and r/ObsidianMD.

### 🛠️ Development & Support (Not Marketing)

* **GitHub Discussions:** Contributor hub.
* **Obsidian Forums:** Technical plugin support.
* **Telegram:** Project bot updates.

## 📊 Success Metrics

* **Early Access:** Size of the waitlist for the Obsidian plugin.
* **Engagement:** Comments and shares on the "Abe Retrospective" series.
* **Retention:** Active users of the 4Data prototype.

## 🎣 Zero-User Acquisition

1. **Dogfooding:** "How I use inCybe to build inCybe" (Public changelogs).
2. **The 4Data Hook:** Playable browser prototype to experience the node-system instantly.
3. **Ollama Auto-Detect:** Lowering friction to zero for existing AI enthusiasts.
